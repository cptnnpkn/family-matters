# Silken Spite
*Melee Weapon, artifact (requires attunement)*  

- **Damage**: 1d8 P
- **Properties**: [Finesse](/3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Finesse), Martial, [Vestige](/3-Mechanics/CLI/rules/item-properties.md#Vestige)
